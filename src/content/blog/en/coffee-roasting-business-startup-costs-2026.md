## 1️⃣ Commercial Coffee Roasting Machine Cost (Realistic 2026 Range)

The cost of a commercial coffee roasting machine depends on:

* Drum construction and diameter
* Burner power and heat transfer design
* Automation level
* Thermal stability engineering
* Country of manufacture
  
👉 If you're comparing professional machines, you can explore our models here:

[**Commercial Coffee Roasting Machines**]( https://krafferoasters.com/products/).

### 💰 2026 Price Ranges

* **0.5–1 kg machines → €5,000 – €10,000**
* **3–6 kg machines → €10,000 – €18,000**
* **10–15 kg machines → €18,000 – €30,000**
* **20–30 kg machines → €23,000 – €40,000+**

Premium European and US brands may exceed these ranges, but many professional manufacturers operate within them.

👉 The key factor is not just price — but **engineering quality and thermal stability**.

## 2️⃣ Installation & Infrastructure Costs

This is where many entrepreneurs miscalculate.

Depending on regulations, you may need:

* Industrial ventilation system
* Chimney with proper height
* Gas certification
* Fire safety compliance
* Emission control or afterburner

### 💰 Estimated Cost:

**€5,000 – €30,000+**

⚠️ Always verify local environmental regulations before purchasing your machine.

---

## 3️⃣ Additional Equipment

A complete roasting setup usually includes:

* Destoner
* Industrial scale
* Sealing machine
* Nitrogen flushing (optional)
* Storage bins
* Work tables

Many new roasters forget these — but they are essential.

---

## 4️⃣ Green Coffee Inventory

Your first coffee purchase ties up capital immediately.

### 💰 Typical Range:

**€5,000 – €25,000+**

Depends on:

* Number of origins
* Volume strategy
* Payment terms
* Target market

Green coffee is both **inventory and working capital**.

---

## 5️⃣ Facility Costs

Roasting usually requires industrial zoning.

You must budget for:

* Security deposit
* 3–6 months rent buffer
* Electrical modifications
* Gas & ventilation setup

⚠️ Facility preparation often costs more than expected.

---

## 6️⃣ Branding & Market Entry

Even perfect roasting won’t sell without positioning.

Startup branding includes:

* Logo & packaging design
* Website & e-commerce
* Photography
* Initial advertising
* Sample kits

### 💰 Estimated Range:

**€3,000 – €15,000+**

---

## 7️⃣ Working Capital (The Survival Factor)

This is the most underestimated cost.

You need reserves for:

* Rent
* Utilities
* Gas & electricity
* Inventory replenishment
* Marketing
* Unexpected maintenance

👉 Plan for **at least 3–6 months buffer**

Without working capital, even strong businesses fail.

# How Profitable Is a Coffee Roasting Business in 2026?

Starting a coffee roasting business is exciting.
But the real question serious entrepreneurs ask is:

**How profitable is coffee roasting — really?**

The short answer:
**It can be highly profitable — if structured correctly.**

The long answer depends on:

* Your batch size
* Your sales channel (retail vs wholesale)
* Your green coffee sourcing
* Your operational efficiency
* Your pricing strategy

Let’s break it down with realistic numbers.

---

## ☕ Coffee Roasting Profit Margin Explained

Green coffee is typically the largest variable cost.

In 2026, specialty green coffee prices generally range between:

* **€4 – €9 per kg** (standard specialty origins)
* **€10 – €18 per kg** (premium micro-lots)

After roasting, coffee loses **14–18% weight** due to moisture loss.

So:

* **1 kg green → ~850g roasted coffee**

If you buy green coffee at €7/kg:
Your effective roasted cost becomes roughly **€8.20/kg**.

If you sell roasted coffee at:

* **€18–22/kg wholesale**
* **€28–40/kg retail**

### 📊 Gross Margin Structure

* **Wholesale margin: 40–60%**
* **Retail margin: 60–75%**

That’s before operational costs.

## 📊 Example Scenario 1: Small 6 kg Roastery (Wholesale Focus)

Let’s assume:

* 6 kg professional drum roaster
* 3 roasting days per week
* 4 batches per day
* 18 kg per day average production

### Monthly Production

**18 kg × 12 days = 216 kg roasted coffee**

If sold wholesale at €20/kg:

**216 kg × €20 = €4,320 monthly revenue**

### 💰 Cost Estimate

* Green coffee → ~€1,750
* Utilities (gas + electricity) → €300–800
* Packaging → €300–600
* Rent → €1,000–3,000
* Miscellaneous → €500–1,000

### 📉 Estimated Monthly Expenses

**€3,850 – €7,150**

### 📈 Net Result

* Conservative → break-even / small profit
* Optimized → **€1,000–2,000 net profit**

👉 Wholesale-only models scale slowly but steadily.

---

## 📈 Example Scenario 2: Retail + Direct-to-Consumer Model

Now let’s assume:

* Same **216 kg monthly production**
* **60% retail at €32/kg**
* **40% wholesale at €20/kg**

### Revenue Calculation

* 130 kg retail → €4,160
* 86 kg wholesale → €1,720

### 💰 Total Revenue

**€5,880/month**

With similar costs, profitability increases significantly.

👉 **Retail channels dramatically improve margins.**

---

## 🚀 Scaling Scenario: 12–15 kg Machine

With:

* 4 roasting days per week
* 3 batches per day
* 12 kg batch size

### Monthly Output

**500–700 kg**

At an average selling price of €22/kg:

**600 kg × €22 = €13,200/month revenue**

### 📈 Profit Potential

With efficient operations and partial retail mix:

**Net profit margins: 15–25%**

👉 This is where roasting becomes financially attractive.
